2‑hour fire‑rated safety envelope

  • Vision panels & doors: Certified 2‑hour-rated glazing and doors to preserve visibility and integrity during fire exposure.
  • Insulated sandwich panels: Minimise heat transfer and help maintain breathable conditions inside the cabin.

Redundant power & fail‑safe engineering

  • Jerk‑less battery backup: Seamless operation for 30+ minutes when mains fail.
